Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this website?
This website helps users monitor and track their mental health by offering regular assessments. It provides insights based on the user's responses to a series of mental health-related questions, empowering them to take control of their well-being.
How do I track my mental health progress?
After completing assessments, the website saves your responses and shows your progress over time with visual data, such as line graphs. You can view comparisons of past assessments to understand changes in your mental health.
Is my data safe and private?
Yes, your data is stored securely and only accessible to you.
